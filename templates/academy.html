1. How to Run a Cash Loan Business

Rule of 3: Always keep 30% of your capital in reserve. Never lend out 100% of your cash at once, or you won't be able to handle emergencies.

KYC (Know Your Customer): In Namibia, always verify the client's residential address and a recent payslip or bank statement to ensure they can actually pay back.

2. Avoiding Scammers

  • The Ghost: Never lend to someone without meeting them or seeing their original ID. Digital copies can be faked.
  • Multiple Loans: Check if they already have loans elsewhere. If they owe 5 people, they won't pay you.
  • The "Emergency" Pressure: Scammers use fake urgency to make you skip security checks. Stay calm; follow your process.

3. How to Get Investors

To get an investor (like a bank or a wealthy partner), you must show them your **CSV Reports**. Investors love DATA.

Show them your "Total Expected" vs "Total Approved." If your default rate (people not paying) is less than 10%, you are a "Gold" business for investors.
